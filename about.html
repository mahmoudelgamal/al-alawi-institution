CEO Message

AL Alawi Training Institute is a global Training and Consulting Organization, delivering the solutions, services to enterprises worldwide with a team of 400+ highly accomplished professionals through robust practices. Over the years, the company had established itself as a reliable partner for many corporate in Oman, India, Middle East, South East Asia, and Africa. At AL-Alawi Institute, we believe that contributing value back to the Omani Society is our responsibility. To make a difference to the world outside, AL-Alawi Institute has started a number of initiatives by stepping out from the traditional operational area to the vast arena of Corporate Social Responsibility. Excellency in today's highly competitive workplace demands more than a thorough knowledge of your specialist field. You can rely on Al-Alawi Institute as a trusted business partner. We strongly focus on the overall growth of Omani citizens by volunteering and conducting seminars, training and consulting. The Design of our training programs starts with the definition of user-focused performance objectives and then proceeds to the selection and implementation of strategies appropriate to those objectives We offer a wide variety of course options across many areas. Learn all the training courses from our Experts. Learn any course with us. We know it's your career.

our statement

our mission

We are committed to enable our clients’ lead the market and go off the beaten path of conventional competition, to discover new opportunities in the future market.

our vision

Our vision is to create an environment that enables learning and development for tomorrow. Hence, our new tagline, “Learning for Future” where we aim to innovate ahead of the industry, constantly evolving in new technology, tools, and methodologies to make learning relevant to the community and Oman Vision.

our value

To facilitate the corporate training function to have obsolete flexibility to meet client expectations To help the corporate workforce to understand and measure their skills, capabilities, aptitude & attitude To guide them individually & collectively through a well thought out a mentoring plan until they attain their career goals To strengthen our clients’ resources by delivering consulting, training & intelligent solutions to help them perform efficiency with an edge over their competitors
